Apple Watch haqida

Apple Watch — dunyoning eng mashhur smart soati. iPhone bilan mukammal integratsiya, sog'liq monitoringi va chiroyli dizayn bilan ajralib turadi. O'zbekistonda Apple Watch Series 10 va Watch SE modellari mashhur.

Apple Watch Series 10

Apple Watch Series 10 — Apple ning eng yangi flagman soati. LTPO OLED Always-On ekran, ECG, SpO2, harorat sensori va Crash Detection funksiyalari bilan jihozlangan. 50 metr chuqurlikda suvga chidamli va 18 soatlik batareya muddatiga ega.

Apple Watch SE

Apple Watch SE — Apple Watch ning eng arzon modeli. GPS, yurak urishi monitoringi, SpO2 va qulash aniqlash funksiyalari bilan jihozlangan. iPhone foydalanuvchilari uchun ideal kirish darajasi soati.
